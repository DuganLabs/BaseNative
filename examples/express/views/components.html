Details & Summary

What is BaseNative?

A lightweight component library built on native HTML templates and signals-based reactivity. Zero dependencies, zero build steps, ~120 lines of runtime.

How does font-stretch switching work?

A single BaseNative font family maps three typefaces to different font-stretch values: normal for sans-serif, expanded for serif, and ultra-condensed for monospace.

Is this production-ready?

It is a proof of concept demonstrating what is possible with the modern web platform. Use it as a foundation and adapt it to your needs.
